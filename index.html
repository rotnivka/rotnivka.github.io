Manifesto

Fashion deserves to be studied with the same rigor as architecture, industrial design or engineering.

ROTNIVKA investigates clothing beyond trends. Every textile carries mechanical properties. Every garment reflects technological choices. Every silhouette communicates cultural information. Through essays, case studies and long-form research, ROTNIVKA explores the systems that shape identity through material culture.
