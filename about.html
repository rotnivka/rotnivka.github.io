About

Research before aesthetics.

ROTNIVKA is an independent research practice investigating how materials, engineering, history, psychology and semiotics shape visual identity.

Who is behind ROTNIVKA

Anya Rotnivka

Materials Engineer and independent researcher. Her work explores the relationship between material behaviour, human perception, textile innovation, fashion history and identity systems. Rather than approaching clothing through trends, ROTNIVKA studies fashion as a technological, historical and cultural system.
